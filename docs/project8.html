Wikipedia

Authored By: Denis Smirnov

Wikipedia, a free open source article a collection of information on various different topics, joined the leader board in February of 2004 instantly taking ninth place with a little over 300 million monthly page views. Wikipedia proccedes to hover in the bottom three until 2001 where it finally breaks out and gets into seventh place. After breaking out of the bottom three wikipedia proceeds to go up the rankings overtaking yahoo for fourth place and then getting over taken by Baidu. Wikipedia finished its run with over five billion page views and in fifth place.
